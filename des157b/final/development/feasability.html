Features

• Users can type in their name in the beginning.
• Users can type in answers to questions relating to their own thoughts, experiences, etc. like “What advice would you give to yourself 5 years ago?” or “What would you say to a student who is going through a lot of pressure?” .. questions like these
• A nice suggestion I got was a grid of these thoughts if users just want to see the answers to these questions
• Users can view what other people answered to these questions as they go through the interactive story
