Battle Cats

This is game of battle cats! This is a 2 player game where you must defeat the other
player to remain the superior cat of Alvin's household.
You can scratch and challenge your opponent with your sharp claws,
or use them to defend and heal back some health!
There are three special rules:
If you roll two 1's, the cat will become distracted. Your turn becomes 0.
If you roll doubles, you'll get a THIRD roll!
If you roll 9, well, cats have 9 lives. The cat gods give you +1 to your score!
