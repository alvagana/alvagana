BATTLE
CATS

This is a game of battle cats! This is a 2 player game where you must defeat the other player to remain the superior cat of Alvin's household.
You can scratch your opponent with your sharp claws,
or use them to defend and heal back some health! At each turn, the higher roll wins!
There are three special rules:
- Roll two 1's, the cat becomes distracted. Your roll is ZEROED OUT!
- Roll doubles, you'll get a THIRD roll!
- Roll 9, then the cat gods give you +1 to your score!
